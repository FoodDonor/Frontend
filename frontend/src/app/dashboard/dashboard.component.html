Our Inspiration

The pressing predicament of food scarcity and widespread starvation looms large in modern society, a universally recognised challenge. However, beneath this acknowledged crisis lies an equally unsettling issue: the wastage of precious sustenance. Delving into this concern, we went in pursuit of knowledge with a clear objective - to bridge the gap between those who discard sustenance thoughtlessly and those who desperately seek it.

Our goals:

  • 1 Million+ people fed in a year
  • Have more than 5000 volunteers registered
  • Have more than 1000 resto owners registered
